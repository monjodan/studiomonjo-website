The person behind Studio Monjo — the slower half of a practice that splits between research and the studio.

I'm Jordan — French by birth, based in Seoul. Studio Monjo is the slower half of what I do: making things by hand and writing in Korean.

The notebooks are painted one at a time, stitched with red thread, and never repeated. The essays are a way of thinking out loud in a language that isn't mine — on consciousness, control, aesthetics, and what it means to live between cultures.

The practice is intentionally small. A few notebooks a month, an essay when one is ready. No rush, no editions of five hundred.

For research, work, and the rest of what I do, see jordanmonnet.com. To commission a notebook, start here.
