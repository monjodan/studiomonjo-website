# The Robey World

The narrative architecture of the Robey line, decided June 2026. Field Notes proves the model: every edition documents something real, the story carries the page, and the specs live lovingly inside it. Robey's version is stronger in one way — the narrator is in the product.

## The loop (the brand engine)

1. 몬조단 walks Seoul and photographs a real place — a library, a window with falling petals, a balloon against the sky.
2. He paints Robey into that scene. The painting becomes an edition cover.
3. The notebook is sewn by hand, with thread matched to the scene.
4. Robey writes a letter about the place — in French and Korean — and the letter ships with the notebook.
5. Robey cannot write yet. The buyer can. The blank pages are the handover.

Every element of the loop is human, real, and verifiable: the place exists, the photo exists, the hand that painted and sewed is the same hand. This is the "human trace" platform made into a story instead of a claim.

## The voice rules

- **Robey speaks in first person on Robey pages — but never tells the story.** (Revised June 2026: the letter ships WITH the notebook; it is part of the product, not the marketing.) The page carries Robey's greeting, the setting, and the promise: 그날의 이야기는 노트와 함께 가는 편지에 있습니다. Mystery on the page, payoff in the box.
- **The letter text never appears on a product page.** The letters in this file are for printing the cards and for reference only.
- **Robey's register**: simple, curious, slightly formal Korean (합니다체), short paragraphs, no exclamation marks. He notices small things. He doesn't sell.
- **The close of every Robey page**: 노트가 도착하면, 러비의 편지가 먼저 인사를 드립니다. / 그다음 페이지는 당신의 차례입니다. (The letter's own closing line stays inside the letter.)
- **몬조단's voice stays separate**: the maker block remains third-person and quiet. Robey is the world; 몬조단 is the hand. They never blur.
- **The Monjo line stays out of the world entirely** — austere, terse, no character. Its identity device is the 카이에: the French school notebook form 몬조단 learned to write in. The two-register discipline is what makes both lines legible.

## The page structure (four movements, decided June 2026)

1. **러비의 인사** (Robey pages) / **물건의 정체** (Monjo pages) — Robey greets and names the setting without the story; Monjo opens on the 카이에/포켓 identity. Plus provenance (the real photo → the painting) on Robey pages.
2. **물건 (The Object)** — usage identity, not dimensions: the 카이에 that moves desk to desk, the pocket notebook that follows you. Binding, lay-flat, inside details, use scenes. Dimensions appear, but they describe; they don't define.
3. **작업실 (The Studio)** — 몬조단, Seoul, the five steps, the hands.
4. **안심 (Technical reassurance)** — paper, ink behavior (calibrated), options, specs, FAQ. Last, quiet, per the Apple register rule.

Container design: clean and minimal, no painted UI elements (brand book: contents never leak into the container). The illustrations enter as photographed content — macro close-ups of the watercolor per edition.

## Series mechanics

- Editions are numbered and accumulate. Each new edition = a new place Robey discovered, a new letter. The collection question that brings buyers back: 러비는 다음에 어디로 갈까.
- The letters are collectible objects (Field Notes' edition-lore model). The 3종 세트 reframes as 세 장면, 세 통의 편지.
- "아직 글을 쓸 수 없습니다" is a series-level hook — whether Robey ever learns to write is a long-arc decision that belongs to 몬조단 alone. Do not resolve it in marketing copy.
- Place-naming is deliberately half-open: the page says the scene is real ("이 장면은 실제로 있습니다") without naming the spot. Letting readers guess/find the place is an engagement mechanic. (Jordan may choose to name places later — his call per edition.)

## Provenance (revised June 2026 — backstage, never front-stage)

**The real photos never appear on product pages** — Jordan's call: showing the photo pulls people out of Robey's world. The realness lives as one quiet sentence in the 작업실 movement ("표지의 장면은 몬조단이 서울에서 실제로 보고 느낀 작은 순간에서 시작합니다"), where the maker speaks and backstage belongs. On the page, the painting carries the world alone — each Robey page gets a 표지 block with the watercolor close-up (brush marks, water blooms).

The photo→painting pairs are **blog and Instagram content**: the making-of genre, where backstage is expected and earns discovery. One post per edition: the place (unnamed), the photo, the painting, the sewn notebook.

## What does NOT change

The discoverability layer is untouched: keyword-first titles, categories, tags, attributes, calibrated claims, FAQ, specs, the photo system, the pack model. Field Notes itself titles products boringly ("Original Kraft 3-Pack") and tells stories inside. The searchable surface and the world coexist; the world lives in the page body, the letter, and the objects.

## The letters

### Edition 003 — 작은 도서관 (verbatim, ships with the notebook, FR/KR)

안녕하세요.

제 이름은 러비입니다.

어느 날 도시를 걷다가 조금 신기한 곳을 발견했습니다. 책장이 끝도 없이 이어져 마치 책들이 바닥에서 하늘까지 닿아 있는 것처럼 보였습니다. 저는 한참 동안 그곳에 서서 그 모습을 바라보았습니다.

수많은 책 속에는 어떤 이야기와 질문, 그리고 꿈들이 담겨 있을까 상상해 보았습니다. 어떤 이야기는 이미 먼 길을 떠났고, 어떤 이야기는 아직 누군가를 기다리고 있을지도 모릅니다.

저는 아직 글을 쓸 수 없습니다. 하지만 빈 노트도 어쩌면 그런 도서관과 닮아 있다고 생각합니다. 처음에는 조용하지만, 한 장 한 장 채워질수록 조금씩 자신만의 이야기를 품게 되니까요.

이제 이 노트는 당신의 것입니다. 이 작은 노트가 당신만의 이야기들을 담아 두는 공간이 되기를 바랍니다.
