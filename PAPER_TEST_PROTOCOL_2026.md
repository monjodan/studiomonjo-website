# Paper Test Protocol 2026

One work-table session that produces the proof layer for all eight Naver listings, the Naver blog post, and Instagram. Built from verified research (June 2026) into what the Korean fountain-pen / 필사 / pen-drawing niche actually tests, with which instruments, and which proof formats they trust. Companion to NAVER_LISTING_SYSTEM_2026.md.

The principle: test what the niche checks, photograph it the way the niche photographs it, name every instrument, and publish one honest failure. Korean sponsored-review fatigue is structural (under 30% of paid posts disclose), so a maker-run, unedited, named-instrument battery with a visible fail is the cheapest trust available.

---

## 0. Setup rules (these make the results quotable)

- Shoot everything on a phone, and SAY so: device name + 보정 없음 (no correction). This is the disclosure formula of the most-trusted community paper test (iPhone, default camera, no edits).
- Test on actual notebook pages, not loose sheets.
- State conditions on the published sheet: date, indoor temp, humidity (the community knows humidity changes feathering results — stating it signals rigor).
- Label every line on the page: ink name + nib + pen, handwritten. Anonymous "a fountain pen" tests get dismissed.
- Front AND back photo of every test page. The back-of-page shot is the proof shot.

## 1. The instruments (ranked by community recognition)

Already in the atelier:
1. **이로시주쿠 (Iroshizuku)** — the most recognized premium reference ink line in Korea. Use at least two colors; if available, 월야(Tsuki-yo) or 야마부도 doubles as the sheen/테 test ink.
2. **Kakimori nib + dip pen** — recognized connoisseur shorthand (stocked at 블루블랙 펜샵, Korean YouTube reviews exist). Doubles as the accepted wet worst-case application.
3. **BIC Onyx permanent marker** — already passed; maps to the 뒷장 블리딩 stress test. Permanent (유성) passing at 105g is a stronger claim than water-based.
4. **Watercolor light wash** — supplementary 혼합 매체 row only (not part of the standard battery; keep it one row, framed for the drawing crossover).

To buy or borrow (modest list):
5. **EF or F Japanese-nib fountain pen** — Korea is a 세필 market; EF/F lines must LEAD every published sample. M and broader appear only as the wet stress row.
6. **Pelikan 4001 블루블랙** — the conservative baseline every 디시/펜후드 reader knows. State old/new formula.
7. **Parker Quink Blue** — the community's wet-but-clean benchmark ("흐름이 좋은데 실번짐이 없는").
8. **Zebra 마일드라이너** — the reference highlighter; its known failure mode (stroke start/end ghosting through) is exactly what gets checked.
9. **펄 잉크 (e.g. J.Herbin 1670)** — dip/glass pen ONLY (community knows 펄 잉크 clogs fountain pens). Enthusiast showcase row.
10. **One alcohol marker (신한 터치 or 코픽)** — bought to FAIL on camera. See §3.
11. Benchmark papers for the blog post: 토모에리버 52g, 미도리 MD, 모닝글로리 복사지 (gold standard / pencil-and-pen default / budget baseline).

## 2. The battery, in the community's vocabulary

Dealbreaker tier (objective, photographable — these decide purchases):
- **실번짐** (thread feathering, "spiderweb" wicking) — EF/F + wet ink, macro shot of strokes. The #1 rejection reason.
- **뒷비침** (show-through) and **뒷장 배김/블리딩** (bleed-through) — the front/back pair of the fully written page. Pass phrasing buyers search: "실번짐 없음", "뒷비침 없음".
- **건조 시간** — timed smear strip: write a line, finger-swipe at 5/10/20/30초, photograph. No Korean review publishes stopwatch numbers, so this EXCEEDS the local standard while staying instantly readable. If 105g dries slower, say so plainly — "slower dry, zero feathering" is an accepted profile when stated rather than hidden.

Enthusiast tier (the "premium paper" differentiators — this is where Tomoe River earns its crown, and where 105g uncoated can compete):
- **발색** (color rendering), **농담** (shading), **테** (sheen halo — shoot under angled light, "테가 뜬다"), **색분리**, **펄** (shimmer hold — dip pen row).
- Target sentence, the canonical Tomoe River compliment: "잉크 본연의 성질을 잘 받아줍니다."

Taste tier (describe, never score):
- **필기감** on the 사각거림 ↔ 매끄러움 axis. State where the paper sits and who it suits. The community distrusts unqualified "smooth" claims; saying "texture is taste" out loud is itself a credibility marker.
- 사각거림 is proven by SOUND: a 30–60 second close-mic clip of the nib on the page, no music, no talking (ASMR 시필 is the established format; also feeds a "필사 with me" clip).

필사/study crossover additions:
- **형광펜 test**: 마일드라이너 over DRIED fountain-pen ink (no smear) + back-side check.
- **180도 펼침** photo (already exists) and a fully transcribed page, both sides of one leaf, proving 양면 사용.

## 3. The honest fail (do not skip)

Run the alcohol marker. Let it bleed through. Photograph it with the same unedited treatment and publish it next to the passes: "알코올 마카는 비칩니다. 알코올 잉크는 평량과 무관하게 비코팅 종이를 통과합니다."

Verified rationale: blunt negative findings earn recommendations in this community (the harshest paper-roundup posts are the most shared), one scoped failure makes every passing claim believable, and it pre-empts the one bad review an alcohol-marker buyer would otherwise write. One failure only — keep the overall read consistent.

## 4. The register rule (decided June 2026 — this governs everything below)

**The brand sells the experience of writing, not technical paper. The Apple model applies: world-class hardware, quietly stated; the experience leads.** The full battery below is for the bench, the blog, and the reviewers — the product pages receive only its quietest artifacts. A listing must never read like a test document. Three registers:

1. **Product pages (the keynote).** Experience copy as designed in NAVER_LISTING_SYSTEM_2026.md. The paper appears exactly where it already does — one quiet claim, one image, the FAQ — upgraded by the session, never expanded by it. No test grids, no instrument lists, no timed strips, no benchmark names.
2. **The blog (the tech-specs page).** The full battery, every instrument named, the honest fail, the benchmark table. The niche self-serves here; it also catches the 만년필 종이 informational searches. Listings can exist without it; reviewers will find it.
3. **The object (the most Apple move available).** The 시필 카드 — proof as a thing in the box, written by hand, instead of claims on a screen. "Don't describe the paper; put it in their hands."

## 5. Publication order

1. **Naver blog post** in the community reading order: 종이 스펙 → 실번짐 macro → 앞뒤 비침/배김 pair → 건조 strip → 필기감 prose → 발색/농담/테 → 펄 (딥펜) → 형광펜 → 180도 펼침 → the honest fail → benchmark table. Keywords: 만년필 종이, 만년필 노트 추천, 필사 노트.
2. **Detail pages**: swap the hero pair + add the new FAQ lines (one editing pass across all eight files once photos exist).
3. **Instagram**: hero pair + sound clip + 테 macro. Tag stack: 만년필, 만년필노트, 만년필종이, 필사노트, 잉크스와치, 시필, 발색, 글씨스타그램, 펜스타그램.
