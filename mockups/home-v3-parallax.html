The figure

"He arrived in 2025, on a card I made for a friend, and refused to leave."

Each card painted in this edition is a variation on the same scene — the same branch, the same berries, the same small figure, painted slightly differently every time.

The hand

Iroful 75gsm. Platinum Pigment. Nevskaya Palitra. Vermilion ink, hand-stamped, last.

Materials chosen one at a time and never substituted. The same paper from the same paper mill in Tokyo. The same archival ink. The same chop, cut from boxwood in 2024.

"The page is the only thing I make that the buyer finishes."

Forty pages of Iroful 75gsm, fountain-pen ready, no feathering, no bleed. Four pages I paint myself, at the front. The other thirty-six are blank, and waiting.
