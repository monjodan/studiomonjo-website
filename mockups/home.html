Studio Monjo · Seoul

Art that follows
the act of writing.

Studio Monjo makes hand-painted notebooks and letter cards in Seoul. Each design is painted in a run of twenty, hand-numbered, and never repeated.

The work is small, slow, and made for the kind of writer who already knows the difference one good page can make.
