Lotus, No. 002

연꽃, 두 번째 에디션

A6 40 pages Iroful 75gsm Hand-stitched

The second edition. A branch of plum blossom painted in ink, arcing over a red lotus blooming at the base. Each cover is finished with a small vermilion seal, stamped by hand.

Like every Studio Monjo edition, the twenty are variations on a theme. The composition is the same. The marks, the drips, the placement of the seal vary from one cover to the next. Painted in February in the studio in Seongsu, on the same week the first plum blossoms came out along the Han River.

About this copy

This is number 7 of 20. The plum branch sits a touch higher than usual; the seal is stamped slightly off-center, just below the blossom on the left. Signed inside the back cover.
