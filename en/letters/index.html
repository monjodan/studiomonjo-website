How they're made

Small, slow runs.

01 · Design

One image at a time

Each design is worked out on paper before it goes into an edition — a subject, a palette, a composition. Cards often come in pairs or short series.

02 · Paint & bind

Twenty at a time

Each of the twenty covers is painted individually, hand-stitched with Sajou waxed linen thread, and signed. Inside: a single sheet of Iroful, ready for your pen.

03 · Ship

Korea or France

Ordered through the Naver Shop in Korea, or the French shop in Europe. Each card ships with its edition number marked inside.
