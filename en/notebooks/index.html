Notebooks & cards

Hand-painted, hand-stitched, made in Seoul.

Notebooks in two sizes (A6 ₩65,000, A5 ₩95,000), bound cards (₩25,000), and seasonal boxed sets of four (₩85,000). Forty pages of fountain-pen paper inside every notebook. Each design is an edition of twenty. Available on the Naver Smart Store.

How they're made

Painted, stitched, signed.

01 · Painted

Twenty at a time

Each cover is painted individually in ink and watercolour. Same subject across the edition, never the same twice. Branches, leaves, small birds, plum, lotus.

02 · Stitched

Hand-bound

Sewn closed by hand with French waxed linen thread. Forty sheets of Iroful paper inside, the kind that takes ink without feathering or bleed.

03 · Signed

Numbered, signed

Hand-numbered inside, signed under the studio's vermillion seal. When the twenty are gone, the design ends.
