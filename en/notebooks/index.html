Notebooks & cards

Hand-painted notebooks, made one at a time in Seoul.

Notebooks (A6, A5), bound cards, and seasonal boxed sets. Forty pages of fountain-pen paper inside every notebook. Each design is an edition of twenty.

The process

A card, painted from start to seal.

Each cover begins as a blank sheet of Canson. Ink line first, then watercolour, then a small vermillion seal. There's no sketch underneath. The line lands or it doesn't.

Once the cover is dry, the notebook is folded, sewn closed by hand with French waxed linen, numbered inside, signed.

Made of

Four makers, one studio.

Iroful paper inside, from the Sakae mill that also makes Tomoe River. Heavy Canson on the covers. Russian White Nights watercolours. French waxed linen on the spine. See the materials ›
