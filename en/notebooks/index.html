How they're made

Small, slow runs.

01 · Design

One image at a time

I work out each new design on paper before committing it to an edition — ink lines, colour choice, composition.

02 · Paint & bind

Twenty at a time

Each of the twenty covers is painted individually — same subject, never the same twice. Every book is hand-stitched, signed, and numbered.

03 · Ship

Korea or France

Ordered through the Naver Shop in Korea, or the French shop in Europe. Each copy ships with its edition number marked inside.
