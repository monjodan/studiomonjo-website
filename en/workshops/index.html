What you take home

Your first edition.

You make one A6 pocket notebook from start to finish. The paper and the waxed thread come from the same family as Studio Monjo editions. You choose a thread color, make the cover yours, and sign the last page with a date and an edition number.

No experience required. Modest drawing is fine. A quiet white cover is fine too.

The steps

The same path an edition takes.

The stages a notebook passes through in the atelier, crossed together at one table.

01

Paper and ink

Feel the 105 g paper and lay some ink on it. It is the same paper the editions use.

02

Fold and sew

Fold the paper, align it, and sew it with the thread you choose. Red, orange, navy, black. The thread color marks your edition.

03

Make the cover

Draw a line in ink, add a wash of colour. A small motif is enough, and an empty cover is enough too.

04

The colophon

Sign the last page, add the date and your edition number. It goes home wrapped in translucent paper.
