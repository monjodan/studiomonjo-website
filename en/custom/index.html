Slow, considered, yours.

Good to know

A few details.

What kinds of subjects do you paint?
My natural subject is the branch. Plum, lotus, leaves, small birds, fruit. I work in ink and watercolour, with a small studio seal in vermillion. I'll lean into what you ask for, but the work will be in this register. If your subject is far from this language, I'll tell you honestly before you commit.
Can I choose the paper and the thread colour?
Yes. The interior paper is Iroful (the fountain-pen paper from Sakae) by default, and I can suggest other options. The binding thread is Sajou waxed linen and comes in many colours; you choose the one that fits your cover.
How long does it take?
From the moment we agree, usually around two to three weeks for a notebook and one to two for a card. I work slowly. I'll give you a date in the first reply.
Boxed sets as commissions?
Not at the moment. The seasonal boxed set is designed as a whole, four cards in one register; commissioning a single component within it isn't a fit. Boxed sets exist as editions only.
